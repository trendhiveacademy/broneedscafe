Own a BRONEED'S CAFE Franchise!

Are you passionate about coffee and community? Join the BRONEED'S CAFE family and bring our unique experience to your city. We offer comprehensive support, a proven business model, and a beloved brand.

Why Franchise with Us?

  • ✓ Strong Brand Identity: Unique logo & name ("Broneed's Cafe"), consistent theme, color, and design, with a catchy tagline (e.g., "Only Veg, Always Fresh").
  • ✓ Flexible Standard Menu: Fixed signature items across all outlets (e.g., Afgani Momos, Peri Peri Momos) with flexibility for regional add-ons to suit local tastes. Focus on veg-only specialization. Dishes will be adapted and changed according to the franchise location's preferences and market demand.
  • ✓ Optimized Café Layout & Ambience: Consistent interior look across franchises, designed for an Instagram-worthy aesthetic, including indoor and optional garden/outdoor setups.
  • ✓ Comprehensive Training & SOP Manual: Detailed staff training guides (cleaning, customer service, food handling), a precise recipe book with exact measurements and cooking methods, and a quality control checklist. Franchisees and their trained staff will receive a certificate from Broneed's Cafe upon completion of training, validating their expertise.
  • ✓ Advanced Technology & Tools: State-of-the-art POS system for efficient billing, online order application/QR code menu, and customer feedback tablets or forms.
  • ✓ Robust Marketing Toolkit: A starter pack including posters, banners, social media graphics, Instagram-ready packaging, and monthly promotional ideas (e.g., "Momo Monday", Loyalty cards).
  • ✓ Professional Uniform Branding: Staff uniforms with the Broneed's Cafe logo, including branded T-shirts, aprons, and caps.
  • ✓ Complete Franchise Kit: Includes a detailed business agreement, investment & profit plan, and access to a dedicated support contact team (e.g., kitchen consultants, marketing assistance).
  • ✓ Strategic Add-On Revenue Services: Opportunities for merchandise sales (cups, mugs, T-shirts), pre-packed snacks (sauces, frozen momo packs), and catering services or food stalls at events.
  • ✓ Enhanced Customer Engagement: Offerings like free Wi-Fi, feedback discounts, monthly events, or momo challenges to foster community.
  • ✓ Continuous Growth & Upgrades: We are committed to continuous improvement, with many upgrades and enhancements planned as the brand grows and evolves, ensuring our franchisees always stay ahead.
